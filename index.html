Domino Blocks

Place the 1×2 dominos into the 4×4 grid so each 2×2 block meets its target sum. Dominos have two pips (1–6). You can place them horizontally or vertically by selecting a domino then two adjacent cells.

How to play
  1. Select a domino from the pool.
  2. Click two adjacent empty cells (horizontally or vertically) to place it. The first clicked cell receives the domino's first pip, the second clicked cell receives the second pip.
  3. Fill the entire 4×4 grid. The four 2×2 blocks (top-left, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-right) show target sums — your final placement must make each block's cell-sum equal its target.
  4. If you finish and all block targets match, you win.
